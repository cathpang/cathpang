Karmakamet Diner in Bangkok



I planned our lunch at Karmakamet Diner,located near Phrom Phong BTS station, in a little lane

just behind Emporium. Upon stepping into Karmakamet Diner, I was blown away by its rustic charm.

The interior is gorgeous: brick walls, dark wood furnishing, long tables, a view of the garden outside.





On the table there's a Karmakamet ‘newspaper’, and with coasters revealing hand-written letters between Edge and Som.

So much thought put into the interior and design.



Except the photogenic environment, the food of Karmakarmet Diner is of very high standard

We ordered 4 main dishes to share, meatballs spaghetti,clam spaghetti, lamb burger and tomato fusilli



Almost every other table was ordering the signature dessert – The Strawberry In The Clouds

The small cup of strawberry short cake was covered by a balloon of rainbow cotton candy,

the cotton candy is enormous and it attracts everyone's eyeballs





The whole dining experience is wonderful, and it's like you went into a secret world.
